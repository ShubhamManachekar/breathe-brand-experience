# About Us

## Overview
A shared brand story page used by both Shop and Business segments. It narrates the history of "Sawai Fragrances" and "Eze Perfumes" merging.

**Route:** `/about-us`

---

## Layout Structure

### 1. Neo Hero
-   **Headline:** "Where heritage meets modern science."
-   **Visual:** Oil texture.

### 2. Origin Story (Split Card)
-   **Design:** Glassmorphic container.
-   **Content:** Text explaining the merger on left; Animated "Leaf" graphic on right.

### 3. Timeline (Vertical)
-   **Events:** 1965 (Foundation) -> 2024 (Neo-Heritage).
-   **Style:** Central line with alternating text/date placement.
-   **Interaction:** Hovering a year highlights the node.

### 4. Values Grid
-   **Cards:** Passion, Innovation, Partnership, Excellence.
-   **Icons:** Heart, Lightbulb, Users, Award.

### 5. Dual CTA
-   **Action 1:** "Business Solutions" (Primary).
-   **Action 2:** "Shop Home" (Outline).

---

## Design Elements
-   **Decor:** Floating 1965-2024 year markers.
-   **Animation:** `animate-float-slow` on key visuals.
