# Shop Contact & Support

## Overview
A support hub for B2C customers, featuring FAQs and a contact form.

**Route:** `/shop/contact`

---

## Layout Structure

### 1. Hero
-   **Headline:** "We are here to help."
-   **Image:** Support/Lifestyle imagery.

### 2. FAQ Accordion
-   **Component:** Stack of expandable cards.
-   **Behavior:** Clicking a question expands the answer panel (`max-h` transition).

### 3. Contact Form (Left)
-   **Fields:** Name, Email, Subject, Message.
-   **Action:** "Send Message" button.
-   **Feedback:** Toast notification on submit.

### 4. Direct Channels (Right)
-   **List:** Email, Phone, Address with icons.
-   **Business Link:** "Business inquiries?" card pointing to B2B contact.

---

## Colors & Style
-   **Cards:** `card-loom` style (subtle border, white background).
-   **Icons:** Accent color for visual hierarchy.
