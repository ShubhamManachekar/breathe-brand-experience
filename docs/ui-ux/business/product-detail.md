# Business Product Detail

## Overview
The B2B view of the Product Detail Page. Instead of "Add to Cart", it focuses on "Request Quote" and bulk benefits.

**Route:** `/business/products/:model`

---

## Key Differences from Shop PDP

### 1. Buy Box (Right Column)
-   **Header:** "Enterprise Solution" + "B2B Pricing" badge.
-   **Content:** "Contact us for volume pricing and installation services."
-   **Action:** "Request Quote" (Primary Button).
-   **Trust:**
    -   "Free delivery & installation for enterprise orders".
    -   "Extended 3-year warranty".

### 2. Pricing
-   **Hidden:** Specific price is often replaced with "Volume Pricing" or just shown as reference.

### 3. Related Products
-   **Action:** "View Details" instead of price.

---

## Shared Elements
-   **Gallery:** Same interactive 360/image viewer.
-   **Specs:** Same technical data.
-   **Description:** Same content.
