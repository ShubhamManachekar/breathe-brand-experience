# Business Login

## Overview
Enterprise portal access. Features a corporate aesthetic distinct from the B2C login.

**Route:** `/business/login`

---

## Layout Structure

### 1. Visual Side (Left)
-   **Background:** `bg-primary` (Navy).
-   **Content:** "Enterprise Control" headline.
-   **Features:** List with icons (Device management, Reordering, Analytics).

### 2. Form Side (Right)
-   **Header:** "Partner Portal".
-   **Demo Access:** "Auto Fill" button for "Luxe Hotels Group" demo.
-   **Mode Toggle:** Login vs Partner Application.
-   **Application Form (Multi-step):**
    -   Step 1: User Info.
    -   Step 2: Company Info (Industry, Size).
-   **Action:** "Access Dashboard" or "Submit Application".

---

## Interactions
-   **Demo Access:** Pre-fills credentials for B2B demo persona.
-   **Multi-step Signup:** Progressive disclosure for partner application.
